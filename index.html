What you can do on Town Wall.

Not a checklist — the basics done properly, with a local-first spine.

Feed

Global → City → Zone.

See posts from everywhere, narrow to your city, or go fully local to your neighborhood zone.

React, reply, save, share — without the noise.

Posting

Post types that feel native to the town square.

Regular posts, anonymous posts, polls, and media. Choose your zone. Keep it simple.

Chat

Messaging that respects boundaries.

DMs, group chats, and chat requests — plus voice/video calls when you need to talk properly.

Discovery

Find businesses and talent nearby.

Browse local businesses and creators. Map + list views, categories, and verification.

Talent

Local creators deserve local discovery.

Town Wall includes dedicated Talent profiles so musicians, artists, photographers, and creators can be found without needing to fight the feed.

Profiles support a bio, portfolio images, social links, and verification — built to turn “I saw your post” into “I booked you.”

Towny

Help, instantly — without digging through menus.

Meet Towny, the in-app assistant. Ask how features work, where to find things, or how to report something. Towny is there when you need clarity, fast.

It’s designed for guidance and support — not fluff.

Calls

When a message isn’t enough, call.

Voice and video calls are built in — great for quickly coordinating a meet-up, checking details, or just having a real conversation.

Controls are simple: mute, camera toggle, speaker, and call timer — with the same block/report controls you’d expect elsewhere.

Polls

Let the community decide.

Polls make it easy to ask the neighborhood and get signal back — from “Which day works?” to “What should we build next?”

Vote once, see results clearly, and keep decisions grounded in the people actually in your city and zone.

Privacy, clearly.

Town Wall is built around data minimization: collect less, leak less, stress less.

No phone number required. You can use Town Wall without linking your SIM to your identity.

No email required. Your account is based on a username + password.

Anonymous posting option. When you post anonymously, your username isn’t shown publicly.

End-to-end encryption. Messages may be encrypted end-to-end, meaning we cannot read the content of encrypted messages. However, metadata (sender, recipient, timestamp) is stored.
