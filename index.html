Privacy, clearly.

Town Wall is built around data minimization: collect less, leak less, stress less.

No phone number required. You can use Town Wall without linking your SIM to your identity.

No email required. Your account is based on a username + password.

Anonymous posting option. When you post anonymously, your username isn’t shown publicly.

End-to-end encryption. Messages may be encrypted end-to-end, meaning we cannot read the content of encrypted messages. However, metadata (sender, recipient, timestamp) is stored.
